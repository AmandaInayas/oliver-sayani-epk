Biography

28-year-old Americana singer-songwriter Oliver Sayani paints raw pictures with original songs and stories, drawing from experiences from his Appalachian upbringing and a life of rambling. His pedal steel-drenched honky tonk, bluegrass and backwoods blues create an authentic sound that captures the spirit of the mountains and the road.

A graduate of the Jamey Aebersold jazz program, Sayani fronts the Louisville-based band Blind Feline, which released the critically acclaimed album "Kentucky Drifter" in May 2023. The record mixes classic country, swampy rock'n'roll, rockabilly and bluegrass elements into a cohesive storybook about an outlaw drifter.

Award Recognition

Received the Appalachian Artist and Entertainment Award in 2023, recognizing his contribution to regional music and culture.

Radio & Podcast Features

Featured on Red Barn Radio, WB Walker's Old Soul Radio Show, and Ron White's Number Juan Tequila Podcast.

Notable Collaborations

Shared stages with Eddie 9V, Afroman, Dallas Wayne (Sirius Outlaw FM), Ted Russell Kamp, Laid Back Country Picker, and members of Exile & Little Texas.
